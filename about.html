About the Project

Welcome to our modern Audio Guide system! This digital guide has been designed to enhance your art viewing experience with detailed audio descriptions and visual content in multiple languages.

Key Features

  • Multilingual Support

    Full support for German and English, with professionally recorded audio in both languages.

  • Mobile-First Design

    Optimized for all devices with an intuitive, touch-friendly interface.

  • Offline Capability

    Works without internet connection once loaded, perfect for gallery environments.

Accessibility

Our audio guide is designed with accessibility in mind, featuring:

  • High Contrast Mode

    Light and dark themes for optimal readability in any lighting condition.

  • Clear Audio

    High-quality audio recordings with clear narration and adjustable volume.

Technical Details

  • Progressive Web App

    Can be installed on your device for quick access and offline use.

  • Privacy Focused

    No personal data collection, everything runs locally on your device.
